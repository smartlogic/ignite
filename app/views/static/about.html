About Ignite

Five minutes, 20 slides. What would you say? At every Ignite, 16 artists, technologists, thinkers, and personalities will take the stage to answer this challenge.

Ignite was started in Seattle in 2006 by Brady Forrest and Bre Pettis. Since then 100s of 5 minute talks have been given across the world. There are thriving Ignite communities in Seattle, Portland, Paris, and now, right here in Baltimore!
