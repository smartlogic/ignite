Guest Organizer: David Adewumi
Ignite #2 Organizer David Adewumi, Founder and Chief Storyteller of Heekya, leads the overall vision and product strategy for the start-up dubbed the ‘Wikipedia of Stories.’ For the past year, he has been contributing writer for VentureBeat.com, a high profile Silicon Valley technology and venture capital blog. Previously, he led sales & product development for Lomic, Inc. an oil & natural gas software firm. Mr. Adewumi has served in the US Army as an Airborne Infantryman, before being appointed to the US Military Academy at West Point. He attended the Pennsylvania State University, where he pursued a degree in Economics.
