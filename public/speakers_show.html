  • Reading the Bible Again for the First Time by Marcus Borg. Borg, a Lutheran theology professor, lays out some basics for approaching the Bible.

So, even if you went to Sunday School up until confirmation (and not all of us did that), there are some wild and crazy things in and about the Bible that you probably never learned. For example: who wrote it and how? Where can I find the weird stuff? Why might I want to read this thing? Intended for beginners and dilettantes; no prerequisites.
